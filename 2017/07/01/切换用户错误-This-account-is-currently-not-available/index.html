切换用户错误:This account is currently not available

2017-07-01

切换用户报错

[garywu@oldboy202 ~]$ su - apache
This account is currently not available.

原因：apache用户的shell为/sbin/nologin

解决方法：以root身份修改/etc/passwd的shell为/bin/bash
         usermod -s /bin/bash 

重新登录即可。
